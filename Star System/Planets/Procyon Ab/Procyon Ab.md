Xenos is a captured rogue planet, designated Procyon Ab. It did not form within the Procyon system, it formed elsewhere, was ejected from its birth system, drifted through interstellar space, and was eventually captured through a gravitational interaction with the Kairos-Lethe system. This origin defines everything about it.

**Physical parameters:**
- Mass: $4\times$ Earth, $2.39 \times 10^{25}$
- Radius: $1.3\times$ Earth,
- Density: ~$14 \ \text{g/cm}^3$, extraordinarily dense, iron-dominated composition
- Surface gravity: ~$2.4 \text{g}$
- Day length: $58$ hours
- Axial tilt: ~$27-30  \degree$

The planet's extraordinary density reflects its composition, an iron-nickel core comprising approximately $60-70$% of the planet's total volume, far exceeding Earth's proportionally smaller core. This generates an exceptionally strong magnetic field, providing partial shielding from Lethe's ultraviolet output. It also means intense geothermal heat, heavy volcanism, and geologically violent surface conditions driven by iron-rich basaltic flows and active tectonics.
