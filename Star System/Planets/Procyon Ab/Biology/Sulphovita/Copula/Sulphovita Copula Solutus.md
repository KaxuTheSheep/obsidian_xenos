### [[Sulphovita]] Solutus
*Emerges ~320 Million AC*

#### Overview
Solutus is the specialist stage of the Copula lineage and the direct descendant of Incitor populations in the most H₂S-depleted peripheral zones. Where Incitor retained H₂S oxidation as a fallback, Solutus has abandoned it entirely — the first organism on [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]] to completely relinquish a founding metabolic pathway.

#### Abandonment Mechanism
Solutus does not lose H₂S oxidation through any active deletion mechanism — [[Sulphovita]]'s peptide architecture has no genome from which modules can be removed. Instead, abandonment occurs through **distributional marginalisation**. As Ferric coordinase becomes increasingly refined across Incitor generations, iron-coordinated activation progressively dominates the internal peptide distribution. H₂S oxidation modules, no longer contributing meaningfully to energy acquisition, accumulate replication errors that go unselected against. Their representation in the peptide network falls across generations until they drop below the functional threshold required to sustain the pathway. The modules are not erased — they are crowded out, becoming vestigial remnants of insufficient concentration to catalyse meaningful chemistry.

#### Metabolic Strategy
Solutus operates exclusively on iron-coordinated methane oxidation. H₂S encountered in the environment is not metabolised — it passes through membrane transport systems without triggering oxidation pathways. Sulphate remains the terminal electron acceptor and the overall reaction is unchanged, but activation is entirely dependent on Ferric coordinase.

This makes Solutus the most metabolically specialised organism of the Erebian Era — and the most vulnerable to iron scarcity. A drop in Pocket B Fe²⁺/Fe³⁺ availability below the threshold required to sustain Ferric coordinase cycling causes immediate and unrecoverable metabolic failure, with no fallback pathway available.

#### Competitive Position
Solutus dominates the most extreme peripheral zones of Pocket B where H₂S availability is lowest and iron availability is highest. It is outcompeted by Incitor in intermediate zones where H₂S fluctuates sufficiently to make pathway flexibility valuable, and by baseline [[Sulphovita]] in vent-proximal zones where H₂S is abundant. Solutus occupies the narrowest ecological niche of any [[Sulphovita]] lineage — but within that niche it is unrivalled.
