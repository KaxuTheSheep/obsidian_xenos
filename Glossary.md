## Terminology
**AC (Ante Capture)** — Before the capture of [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]] into the [[Procyon]] system. Used as a [[timeline]] marker, analogous to BCE. All dates expressed as {number} AC count backward from capture.

**PC (Post Capture)** — After the capture of [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]] into the [[Procyon]] system. Used as a [[timeline]] marker, analogous to CE. All dates expressed as {number} PC count forward from capture.

## Eras
**Ignic Era** — The first era of [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]'s history, during which the planet formed and existed within its birth system around an unknown red dwarf star. Characterised by geological formation, cooling, and the establishment of early crustal and hydrological conditions. No life present.

**Erebian Era** — The second era, beginning with [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]'s ejection from its birth system and ending at capture. Characterised by complete darkness, global ice coverage, subsurface liquid water maintained by geothermal heat, and the emergence of the first life in deep hydrothermal vent systems. Named for Erebus, the Greek primordial deity of darkness.

**Photian Era** — The third era, beginning at the moment of capture into the [[Procyon]] system. Light arrives on [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]] for the first time. Characterised by atmospheric transformation, surface melting, and the explosive diversification of life. Named for Phos, the Greek primordial deity of light. Currently unnamed beyond this point.

## Biology
**[[Ferrovita]]** — One of the two founding domains of life on [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]. Mineral-based, iron-sulphur lattice organisms inhabiting the deepest hydrothermal vent zones. Emerged independently during the Erebian Era. See [[Ferrovita]].

**[[Sulphovita]]** — One of the two founding domains of life on [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]. Peptide-based, sulphur-chemistry organisms inhabiting the peripheral hydrothermal vent zones. Emerged independently during the Erebian Era. See [[Sulphovita]].

**[[Firmus Ferrovita]]** — The ancestral sub-lineage of [[Ferrovita]]. Occupies the deepest, hottest vent zones. Primary metabolism is iron reduction. See [[Firmus Ferrovita]].

**[[Novus Ferrovita]]** — The derived sub-lineage of [[Ferrovita]], descended from Firmus. Occupies cooler vent margins. Primary metabolism is hydrogen oxidation. Represents the first divergence event in [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]'s biological history. See [[Novus Ferrovita]].

## [[Geology]]
**Pocket A** — The fluid environment associated with [[Ferrovita]]. Strongly acidic (pH 1–2), iron-dominated, high temperature (150–250°C). See [[Hydrology]].

**Pocket B** — The fluid environment associated with [[Sulphovita]]. Moderately acidic (pH 3–4), sulphur and methane rich, lower temperature (50–120°C). See [[Hydrology]].

**Primary Crust** — The dense iron-rich basaltic crust covering the majority of [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]]'s surface. Continuously recycled by tectonic activity.

**Volcanic Highlands** — Elevated platforms built from prolonged shield volcanic accumulation. The only significant landmasses on [[Procyon Ab|Xenos]].
